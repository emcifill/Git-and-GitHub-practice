Rainforest Animals

Here are some lesser-known rainforest animals.

  • Okapi

    • Just like the giraffe and cow, the okapi has four stomachs that aid with digesting tough plants.

    the okapi has a long, dark tongue that can strip leaves from branches

  • Glass Frog

    • A primary fact about glass frogs is their transparent skin, which allows their internal organs, like the heart, to be visible

    Glass Frogs Are Found Only in South and Central America

  • Sun Bear

    • Sun bears are the world's smallest bear

    They possess exceptionally long tongues for extracting honey
